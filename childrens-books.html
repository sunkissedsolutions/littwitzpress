The Moon Lantern
by Loreley Amiti  & Simone Stanghini

So far, this first night in the new house has been awful and Layla’s present, an ugly old lantern, doesn’t cheer them up at all. When they discover its magic though, everything changes and maybe Mummy was right, after all…

Halloween Holler
by Loreley Amiti  & Simone Stanghini

Or so he claims… On the night of Halloween, he plans to overthrow the feared dogs of The Unspeakable South by summoning a mighty cat army. But Fergus, a rough dog from the other side of the river, has the same plan and suddenly, things go quite wrong.
